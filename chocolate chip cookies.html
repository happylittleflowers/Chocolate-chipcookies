Chocolate Chip Cookies

Let's learn how to make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup + 2 t flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream butter or margarine and sugar with the electric mixer until mixture is light and fluffy, not chunky.
  3. Beat in the eggs and the vanilla.
  4. Unplug the electric mixer and put the beaters in the dishwater to be washed.
  5. Using the wooden spoon, stir in the dry ingredients.
  6. Add the chocolate chips. Mix well.
  7. Drop by spoonfuls onto a parchment lined cookie sheet.
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes.
